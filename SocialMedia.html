Social Media Background Check to Gain Insight into a Candidate's Online Presence

Social Media Check

It involves researching a prospect's social media profiles and their activity, including what they post, like, and comment on. Some of the platforms they are likely to check our LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.

Generally, employers are looking for any red flags. These could include the sharing of illegal activities, offensive comments, violent or aggressive behaviour, sexually explicit material, or confidential information.

Here are the steps involved in Tekspy's social media check process

Social Media Profile Review and Analysis

We review the candidate's social media profiles and activity, including posts, comments, and interactions.

Social Media Review and Report Compilation

The employer or verification company compiles a report based on the information gathered from the candidate's social media accounts. The report may include details about the candidate's online activity, including any posts or comments that may be relevant to the hiring process.

Social Media Profile Verification and Report

The employer uses the report to make a hiring decision or to confirm the candidate's social media activity as part of the onboarding process.

Benefits of Social Media Check

Ensures Accuracy and Reliability of Candidate Information

Ultimately, a Social Media Background Check is an important tool for employers to gain a deeper understanding of a candidate, beyond just their professional qualifications and experience. By reviewing their social media activity, employers can get a better sense of how a candidate conducts themselves in their daily life, and whether they align with the values and expectations of the company. This information can be crucial in making informed hiring decisions and creating a productive and positive work environment.

Tekspy is a background verification service that helps employers verify the accuracy of information provided on job applicants' resumes and in their previous employment history.
