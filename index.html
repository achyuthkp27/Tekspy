RESUME VALIDATION

In Today's aspiring world, recent surveys state that most IT industry candidates lie on their resumes.

  • The employers provide the employee's resume to us as a part of the verification process.
  • We review the resume to identify any potential discrepancies or inconsistencies in the provided information.
  • Our team may verify the candidate's employment history by communicating with their previous employers to confirm the positions held and dates of employment.

This process helps ensure that the candidate's provided information is accurate and reliable, which can be important for a variety of purposes, such as evaluating their qualifications and suitability for the role. It can also help protect the employer from potential liability or other issues that may arise from hiring an employee with false or misleading information on their resume.

EDUCATION CHECK

One aspect of the education verification process involves checking for any gaps in a candidate's education history. This can be important for companies to know, as education gaps may raise questions about a candidate's work history and commitment to their studies.

Another aspect of the education verification process involves checking for fake documents. Some candidates may provide forged or altered documents to misrepresent their educational history.

  • We verify education history through public records searches and direct contact with educational institutions.
  • We confirm education by directly contacting the listed institution.
