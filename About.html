About Us

At Tekspy, we respect our clients' privacy and take confidentiality seriously, allowing us to consistently provide top-notch background verification services that earn us repeat business and referrals.

Tekspy is a background verification service that helps employers verify the accuracy of information provided on job applicants' resumes and in their previous employment history.

This includes verifying details such as an applicant's job titles, dates of employment, and the projects they worked on at previous jobs. By performing these checks, Tekspy aims to help employers identify and exclude candidates who may not have the necessary skills or experience for a particular position.

This can be particularly useful in industries where a strong skill set is critical to success, such as in technical or professional roles.

Who we are?

The Quick-Witted Employee Background Verification Company

Tekspy is a company committed to providing the best background authentication services to enhance the evolution of its clients' companies. With a team of young seers, Tekspy takes a personal approach to verifying the skills, education, and experience of candidates to ensure accurate employee authentication data for its clients.

Our Process

Tekspy recognizes the importance of technology in today's world and leverages it in its verification process. However, the team also takes a personal approach to candidate verification, ensuring that only those with genuine skills and backgrounds are considered for employment.

Our team

Although Tekspy currently has a small team, it is dedicated to helping employers with their resource authentication needs. By providing accurate employee data, Tekspy saves its clients time and money in their recruitment processes.

Conclusion

Tekspy's combination of personal attention and technological know-how make it the perfect resource for any company looking to enhance its evolution through effective employee background verification.
