In Today's aspiring world, recent surveys state that most IT industry candidates lie on their resumes.

Resume Validation

To avoid those, Tekspy spring up with a unique service to validate a resume, with this service we thoroughly verify the information on a candidate's resume, and we can provide a genuine report in a short amount of time.

Overall, it's important for companies to be thorough and vigilant during the resume validation process in order to identify and eliminate fake candidates.

Here are the steps involved in Tekspy's resume validation process

Resume Verification Process

The employers provide the employee's resume to us as a part of the verification process.

Resume Review

We review the resume to identify any potential discrepancies or inconsistencies in the provided information.

Employment History

Our team may verify the candidate's employment history by communicating with their previous employers to confirm the positions held and dates of employment.

Educational Qualifications

In addition, we verify the candidate's educational qualifications by getting in touch with the schools or universities listed on the resume.

Reference Checks

Our experts also conduct reference checks to confirm the candidate's character and work ethic.

Project Verification

We went through the projects of the candidate mentioned in the resume, they are undergoing or not by project check with our expert team.

Candidate Resume Verification Reportn

Once the verification process is complete, we will prepare a report on the candidate's resume, indicating whether the information provided has been genuine or not.

Informed Hiring Decisions with Verification Report

The employer can use this report to help make a more informed hiring decision.

Benefits of Resume Verification

Ensures Accuracy and Reliability of Candidate Information

The resume verification process helps ensure that the information provided by the candidate is accurate and reliable, which is important for evaluating their qualifications and suitability for the role. This process protects the employer from potential liability or issues that may arise from hiring an employee with false or misleading information on their resume.

Tekspy is a background verification service that helps employers verify the accuracy of information provided on job applicants' resumes and in their previous employment history.
